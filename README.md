## University Fixed Asset Management System

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/0861faf82f6f422982b7bc2eac749a42/ghb20250916) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`.
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript` etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can run)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular User (Student), Procurement Staff, Employee, Administrator.



**User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Fixed Asset Management
6. Borrowing Information Management
7. Collection Information Management
8. Asset Repair Request Management
9. Asset Maintenance Management




**Procurement Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Fixed Assets
6. Asset Procurement

**Employee Functions:**
1. Login
2. Register
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Fixed Asset Management
6. Asset Inventory Management
7. Asset Counting Management
8. Asset Maintenance Management
9. Borrowing Information Management
10. Collection Information Management
11. Asset Repair Request Management
12. Asset Maintenance Management
13. Asset Disposal Management
14. Asset Transfer Management





**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center (Change Password, Personal Information)
3. Student Management
4. Procurement Management
5. Employee Management
6. Asset Category Management
7. Fixed Asset Management
8. Asset Inventory Management
9. Asset Counting Management
10. Asset Maintenance Management
11. Borrowing Information Management
12. Collection Information Management
13. Asset Repair Request Management
14. Asset Maintenance Management
15. Asset Disposal Management
16. Asset Procurement Management
17. Asset Transfer Management
